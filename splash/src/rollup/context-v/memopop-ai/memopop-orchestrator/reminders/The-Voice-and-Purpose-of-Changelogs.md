Lead with impact — punchy synopsis, story-shaped Why, structured What Changed with

  Why Care?


 1. make us proud, 
 2. other people in our firm and our clients (technical and non) can see what we're up to and get the impression we are fun, relatable, effective, nerdy, bleeding-ege, and willing to share, 
 3. potential customers getting a feel for us, and 
 4. random nerds who search for how to do something and happen to find us, like just below stack overflow.
