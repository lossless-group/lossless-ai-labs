## Why Care?

Every deck, memo, enriched record set, and agent answer we ship is only as good as the corpus behind it — and corpus assembly has now shown up as a sub-feature in at least three products: augment-it's corpus inbox and funder corpora, memopop's research inputs, and dididecks' substantiation corpora. When the same shape recurs that many times, the tree's answer is to give it a home of its own. `corpora-builder` is that home: a project whose whole job is capturing, triaging, fetching, filing, and quality-checking source material.

## What's New?

- The repo exists: `lossless-group/corpora-builder`, mounted as a submodule of `ai-labs`.
- Universal directories scaffolded per the pseudomonorepos convention: `context-v/` (all six subdirectories) and `changelog/` as root siblings.
- A `splash/` slot reserved per `maintain-splash-pages` — **known follow-up**: the Astro splash itself is not yet scaffolded; Phase 1 ships without it.
- First design work underway: `context-v/explorations/Corpora-Builder-System-Design.md`, which surveys the prior art (augment-it's Build-Corpora flow, the source-curation gate blueprint, the context-vigilance Chroma ingest) before any code gets written.

## What's Next?

The exploration hardens into a spec once the option space settles — domain model, capture surfaces, curation gate, and the consumption contracts (Chroma ingest, deck/memo grounding). Patterns will travel to sibling apps knots-style — blueprint plus copy-from sample code — never as a shared package.
